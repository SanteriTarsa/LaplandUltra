Luxury Lapland Holidays: How Lapland Ultra Designs Private Arctic Journeys

Search for “luxury Lapland holidays” and you'll find no shortage of group tours, fixed departures, and shared itineraries. Lapland Ultra was built for travellers who want something else: a private Arctic journey designed entirely around them, rather than a seat on someone else's bus. We are not a travel agency selling packages off a shelf — we are a private concierge, curating and, where wanted, personally accompanying bespoke journeys through Finnish Lapland for international travellers.

That distinction matters. A conventional operator sells lapland luxury tours as fixed products — same route, same hotel, same schedule for every guest. Lapland Ultra instead starts with a conversation: your dates, your party, and what draws you to the Arctic, then composes an itinerary from scratch around it, with accommodation, guides, and pacing built in from the start.

What a Luxury Lapland Holiday Includes

At the centre of every luxury Lapland itinerary we build is the Northern Lights — private aurora expeditions timed to the forecast rather than a fixed departure hour, led by guides who know exactly where the sky is likely to open on a given night. Around that, we layer in snowmobile safaris across untouched snowfields, quiet evenings among reindeer herds, and, for many guests, a night's stay beneath a glass ceiling, watching the aurora from bed in one of Lapland's glass igloo suites.

Accommodation is arranged with the same care: private villas with their own saunas and fireplaces, and a small number of luxury hotels we return to season after season for their setting and service. None of it is assembled from a catalogue — every stay, guide, and vehicle is chosen and confirmed by Lapland Ultra directly, so the itinerary holds together as one journey rather than a series of separate bookings.

Where Lapland Ultra Takes You

Lapland Ultra is rooted in Rovaniemi, the gateway to Finnish Lapland, and reaches out from there into Saariselkä, Levi, and Pyhä, each offering its own terrain, light, and pace. For guests who want to go further still, we also arrange journeys to Kuusamo and Kilpisjärvi, two of the region's more remote and rarely visited corners, where the skies are darker and the wilderness is quieter.

Which destination suits a particular trip depends on the dates, the forecast, and what you're hoping to see — which is exactly why we plan destination by destination rather than defaulting to one fixed route. More travellers are now searching for luxury holidays Lapland can genuinely deliver privately, rather than as part of a shared coach tour, and the region's geography is part of why: no two corners of Finnish Lapland offer quite the same combination of light, landscape, and access.

Designing Your Own Journey

Every Lapland Ultra itinerary begins the same way: you tell us your dates, your party, and what's drawing you to the Arctic — the Northern Lights, the wilderness, a milestone worth marking somewhere remote. From there we compose a private plan around it, confirm accommodation and guides directly, and stay involved throughout the trip so that if the forecast or your plans shift, the itinerary can shift with them.

A luxury Lapland holiday, arranged privately, isn't really about any single experience — it's about having someone design the whole journey around you and stand behind every part of it. That's the role Lapland Ultra plays, from the first inquiry to the last night under the aurora.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a Lapland holiday "luxury" with Lapland Ultra?

It means the itinerary is designed privately around you rather than sold as a fixed package — your own guide and vehicle, accommodation chosen from villas, luxury hotels and glass igloos, and a plan that can change with the weather or your preferences as the trip unfolds.

Which parts of Lapland does Lapland Ultra cover?

We are rooted in Rovaniemi and arrange journeys across Saariselkä, Levi, Pyhä, Kuusamo and Kilpisjärvi, choosing the destination — or combination of destinations — that best suits your dates and interests.

Do you only arrange Northern Lights tours?

No. Northern Lights expeditions are central to most itineraries, but we also arrange snowmobile safaris, reindeer experiences, glass igloo and villa stays, and complete multi-day journeys that combine several of these into one private trip.
