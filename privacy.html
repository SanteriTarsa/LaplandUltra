Privacy Policy

Lapland Ultra · Bespoke Arctic Experiences

Lapland Ultra ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you use laplandultra.com, how we use it, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our website, we collect the information you provide directly, which may include your name, email address, desired travel dates, number of guests, accommodation preferences, and any other details you choose to share about your trip.

We also use standard web technologies to understand how visitors reach and use our site, including advertising conversion tracking (see "Cookies & Advertising" below).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and discuss your travel plans
  • Design and coordinate your itinerary with our accommodation and activity partners
  • Communicate with you before, during, and after your journey

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers the inquiry form submissions to us. Formspree's own privacy practices apply to data in transit through their platform.
  • Google Ads — we use conversion tracking to understand which advertising leads to inquiries. This may involve cookies or similar technologies placed by Google. You can learn more about Google's practices, and manage your ad preferences, at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

When we arrange your itinerary, we share the relevant details of your inquiry (such as your name, travel dates, and preferences) with the specific hotels and activity partners involved in your trip, solely for the purpose of fulfilling your booking.

Data Storage & Retention

Your information is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, coordinate your journey, and meet any applicable legal or accounting obligations.

Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please reach out via our inquiry form, and we will respond as soon as possible.
