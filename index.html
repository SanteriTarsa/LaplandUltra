The Experience

Lapland Ultra curates private journeys into Finland's Arctic wilderness — Northern Lights expeditions, snowmobile safaris across untouched snowfields, and evenings among reindeer herds beneath a sky few ever see. Every itinerary is composed individually, from glass igloo nights to the region's most exceptional lodges, for travellers who prefer discretion to spectacle.

Signature Journeys

Northern Lights, Privately
Aurora expeditions away from the crowds, timed to forecast and led by guides who know exactly where the sky will open.
Arctic Wilderness
Snowmobile safaris, husky and reindeer encounters, and cross-country routes through Lapland's deep snow, tailored to your pace.
Glass Igloos & Luxury Villas
Fall asleep beneath the aurora in a private glass suite, or unwind in one of Lapland's finest villas and luxury hotels — reserved and arranged entirely on your behalf.
Across Lapland
Rooted in Rovaniemi, with private itineraries reaching Saariselkä, Levi, and Pyhä — and, for those seeking further discretion, rarely offered destinations such as Kuusamo and Kilpisjärvi.
